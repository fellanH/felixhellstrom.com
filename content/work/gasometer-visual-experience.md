## The challenge

Gasometer is a 1,740-seat theater and event venue in central Stockholm, operated by Cirkus Venues. They needed a website that matched the scale and atmosphere of the physical space — something that felt immersive, not just informational.

## What I built

A dark, cinematic Webflow site with scroll-driven animations and full-bleed imagery. The design treats the venue itself as the hero — large-format photography, ambient color palettes, and transitions that mirror the experience of walking into the space.

Every interaction was intentional. Scroll-triggered reveals, parallax layers, and subtle motion give the site a sense of depth without sacrificing performance or accessibility.

## Craft details

- **Scroll-driven animations** built with Webflow interactions — no external libraries
- **Dark cinematic palette** that matches the venue's atmosphere
- **Full-bleed imagery** with optimized loading and responsive breakpoints
- **Minimal UI chrome** — the venue is the content
- **Performance-first** — animations are GPU-accelerated, images are properly sized

This project was about restraint as much as ambition. The best visual experiences don't overwhelm — they guide attention. Every animation serves the story of the space.
