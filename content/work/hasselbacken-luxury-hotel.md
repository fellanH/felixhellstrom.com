## The challenge

Hasselbacken is one of Sweden's most storied venues — a luxury hotel, restaurant, and event space on Djurgården in Stockholm. The existing website didn't reflect the prestige of the brand or the breadth of their offering.

## What I built

A full Webflow rebuild with a cinematic video hero, smooth page transitions, and booking integrations for hotel rooms, restaurant reservations, and event inquiries. The site supports multiple languages and balances visual richness with fast load times.

The design leans into the venue's heritage — warm tones, generous whitespace, and large-format photography that lets the spaces speak for themselves. Every section was built as a reusable CMS module so the Hasselbacken team can update content without developer involvement.

## Technical depth

- **Video hero** with responsive sources and lazy-loaded fallback images
- **Booking integrations** for hotel, restaurant, and events — each with distinct flows
- **Multi-language** content structure in Webflow CMS
- **Client-First** class architecture for long-term maintainability
- **Performance** — optimized media delivery through Vidzflow CDN

The project required close collaboration with the client's marketing team to ensure the site captured the right tone — luxurious but approachable, historic but modern.
